About me

I’m a ketchup-loving, anime-watching workaholic, who loves meddling in various fields of design to draw inspiration from people and products around the globe and across various cultures.

Who?

From India's pink city (Jaipur), my name is Bhumika, but you can simply call me Bhoom.

I am fascinated with various facets of humans and designs, which has certainly helped expand my knowledge of how various preferences and behaviors have shaped products all over the world. Having worked in India, US and UK with clients from diverse business. regional and cultural backgrounds via many different fields of design (Industrial, Packaging, Audio GUI, UI, UX), I’ve learnt that I passionately enjoy working with people to reach a shared goal. This can often take many forms of design - Invisible, vibrant and very many things, but they all share the common goal of solving a problem. And that’s what I look forward to as a designer.

When I’m not working away, I’m out in the nature- hiking, out for a ride on my bike or simply spending some me-time with my loved ones.
