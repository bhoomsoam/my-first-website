Emergency Contact Sign-up Experience

Role

Product Designer (Worked in a team of 2)

Client

A B2B company aiming to empower people through technology to help create safer organizations.

Goal

This was a UI redesign project aimed at improving the web-based sign-up process for the Emergency Contacts invited by the ROAR Personal Safety app users.

Through research, we learned that often the contacts that were invited were looking for more clarity about what they were signing up for and wanted to be able to access that information as and when required in the sign-up process. Thus we came up with an enhanced web experience to meet their needs.

Original sign-up process

Key findings

  1. The user flow should be interactive and simple yet informative.
  2. The invited contact should have enough information on the product, their role and be able to clarify any concerns with ease, to make a decision comfortably.
  3. The user should be able to identify how far along in the process they are. They should also be able to easily access any information needed after the sign-up process is complete.
